About Me

Dedicated and enthusiastic professional with seven years of experience managing complex projects and leading cross-functional teams.

Currently work as a Client Success Manager with a focus on enterprise web delivery for our 90+ utility clients. Opower's technology platform analyzes more than 300 billion meter reads to deliver its services, and has created enough energy savings through behavior change to power all the homes in a city of 1 million people for a year.
